Data Notes

Data collection window: May 2017 to August 2017. Records are compiled from major organizational directories and validated through websites/news sources.

Codebook type definitions: Community Service Organization, Advocacy (advocacy organizations), Hybrid (both).

Current CSV totals: 299 Asian organizations and 519 Latino organizations.

Learn more about the data: Kim, Jae Yeon, 2019, "Asian American and Latino Advocacy and Community Service Organizations (1868-2016)", Harvard Dataverse, V6 .

If you want to learn about the related book project, Unseen and Uncounted, please visit: https://jaeyk.github.io/book_projects/books.html .
